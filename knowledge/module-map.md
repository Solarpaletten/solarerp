# Solar ERP Module Map

Solar ERP consists of several functional domains.

## Core

- companies
- users
- authentication
- onboarding

## Master Data

- clients
- products
- employees
- chart-of-accounts
- vat-rates
- operation-types

## Documents

- purchases → inventory increase + accounts payable
- sales → accounts receivable + revenue recognition

## Warehouse

- warehouses
- warehouse movements
- warehouse balances

Warehouse movements are generated by:

- purchases
- sales
- internal transfers

## Inventory Control

- stock valuation
- FIFO layers
- cost of goods sold

## Accounting

- journal entries
- FIFO inventory valuation
- reposting
- accounting periods
- financial reports

## Reports

- balance sheet
- profit and loss
- trial balance
