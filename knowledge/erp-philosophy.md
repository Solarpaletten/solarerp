# Solar ERP Philosophy

Solar ERP follows strict accounting and inventory principles.

The system is designed to guarantee financial correctness, inventory accuracy and auditability.

---

## Accounting Philosophy

Solar ERP follows classical double-entry accounting.

Rules:

- every financial transaction must create balanced journal entries
- total debit must always equal total credit
- accounting entries are generated by business documents

Accounting logic is implemented inside:

lib/accounting/journalService

---

## Inventory Philosophy

Inventory is treated as a financial asset.

Rules:

- every warehouse movement must affect inventory valuation
- inventory valuation must follow FIFO
- cost of goods sold must be derived from FIFO layers

Inventory logic is implemented inside:

lib/accounting/fifoService
lib/accounting/stockService

---

## Document Lifecycle Philosophy

Business documents drive system state.

Documents follow lifecycle stages:

draft → posted → accounting → warehouse

Rules:

- draft documents do not affect accounting
- posted documents generate accounting entries
- posted documents become immutable

---

## ERP Invariants

Solar ERP guarantees the following invariants:

- double entry accounting
- tenant isolation
- FIFO inventory valuation
- accounting period locking
- document immutability

These invariants ensure financial integrity and auditability of the system.
